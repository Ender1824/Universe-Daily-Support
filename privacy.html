Privacy Policy (Version 1.0)

Universe Daily iOS App

Effective Date: 1 September 2025

The Universe Daily iOS app values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

The Universe Daily iOS app does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. No personally identifiable information (such as names, emails, or device identifiers) is gathered.

Internet Connection

The app connects to the internet solely for the purpose of retrieving and displaying relevant data (such as space- or science-related information). This connection is used only to provide content within the app.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media platforms.

Changes to This Policy

If future updates require changes to how data is handled, this Privacy Policy will be updated and a new version number will be issued.
